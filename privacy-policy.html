Privacy Policy

Hasba AI — Voice Calculator by Dezel Labs

Last updated: March 26, 2026

1. Introduction

Dezel Labs ("we", "us", or "our") built the Hasba AI app as a free, ad-supported application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect, store, or share any personal data.

Hasba AI is designed with your privacy in mind. Here is a breakdown of the permissions we request and why:

  • Microphone Access: The app uses your device's microphone solely to convert your spoken words into mathematical expressions using the device's built-in Speech-to-Text (STT) engine. Your voice data is processed locally on your device or via the operating system's speech recognition service. We do not record, store, or transmit your audio to any external server.
  • Internet Access: Required for loading advertisements and for the optional Newton API mathematical processing service. No personal data is transmitted.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use an advertising identifier (AD_ID) to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. This identifier is not linked to your personal identity. For more details, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Speech-to-Text: When you use voice input, the device's built-in speech recognition service may process your audio. This is handled entirely by your device's operating system and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Storage

Hasba AI stores your calculation history locally on your device only using SharedPreferences. This data is never uploaded to any server and is deleted when you uninstall the app or clear the app data.

5. Children's Privacy

Hasba AI is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal data at all, no special measures are needed.

6. Security

We value your trust. Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, there is minimal risk to your privacy. All computation is performed locally on your device.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
